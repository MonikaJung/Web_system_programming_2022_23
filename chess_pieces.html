Let's go over how to set up a chess board. Light pieces starting positions Dark pieces starting positions
King The King moves from its square to a neighboring square One square left open for the King
Queen The Queen may move like a Rook or a Bishop Light Queen sits on a light square Dark Queen sits on a dark square
Rook/Castle The Rook can move in its line or row 2 Rooks (Castles) go in the corner
Bishop The Bishop moves diagonally 2 Bishops go next to the Knights
Knight The Knight jumps in making the shortest move that is not a straight one 2 Knights (not Horses) sit next to Rooks
Pawn The Pawn moves one square straight ahead 8 Pawns go in second to last row
